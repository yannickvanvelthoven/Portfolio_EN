Yannick Van Velthoven lives in Antwerp and started studying Illustration at Sint-Lucas Antwerp in the year 2012. After three years he decided he had developed a growing interest in typography and graphic image-making. He left Antwerp in 2015 and decided to study Graphic Design in Ghent, starting in 2015. He will graduate at the end of June in 2017.

His work is easily describable as pure, clean and minimal. As he says; "Concept defines form.". This can be seen throughout his entire print and digital portfolio. No form can be too pure, you'll never notice added gimmicks.

As he comes to the end of his long studying career, he hopes to meet possible clients in the field of art and cultural publications, as it makes up his biggest field of interest. He hopes to grow to be one of the main players in the field of graphic design.
